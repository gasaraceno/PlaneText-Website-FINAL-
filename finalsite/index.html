Why PlaneText?

Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University published an article regarding the correlation    between language barriers and miscommunication between Air Traffic Controllers and Pilots. FlightLang is meant to break that barrier, to assist in lowering the amount of incidents caused by miscommunication. Air Traffic Controllers and Pilots worldwide can connect and converse in their native tongue, with no need to worry about being misinformed. You can read more about the article here.

TranslationTC was developed for HackRiddle2K17 by Fadi Bitar and Gabriele Saraceno. Happy Hacking!
